Seller tips

4 ways Toyota sellers get top dollar

Our team helps Toyota owners sell every day. Here's what the top sellers do differently.

🏆

Let dealers compete — don't accept the first offer

The spread between the lowest and highest bid for the same Toyota can be $2,000–$6,000. BidBus surfaces all competing bids automatically so you never leave money on the table.

↑ Typical advantage: 18%–34% above first offer
📋

Gather your Toyota's service records before listing

A documented maintenance history — especially for Tacomas, 4Runners, and Prius models — can add $500–$2,500 to your final bid. Dealers pay a premium for cars they can trust.

↑ Typical boost: $500–$2,500
📸

Take great photos — they drive dealer bids

Dealers rely entirely on your listing photos to determine their top offer. Clean, well-lit photos of interior, exterior, and odometer consistently drive bids 12–18% higher.

↑ Photo quality impact: up to 18% higher bids
⏱️

Set a realistic reserve price to maximize competition

A reserve price that's too high discourages bids. Our agents will help you set the right floor — one that protects your interests while encouraging maximum dealer participation.

↑ Optimal reserve = more competing bids
